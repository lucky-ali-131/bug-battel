About BugFix Battle



What is BugFix Battle?

BugFix Battle is a coding practice platform for diploma students where challenges involve identifying and fixing buggy code. Admins upload real coding problems, and students attempt to submit correct fixes.



Our Mission

To sharpen debugging skills by providing hands-on experience with broken code snippets, encouraging practical learning and logical thinking.

Who Can Use This?

  • Diploma and undergraduate students
  • Teachers who want to assign challenges
  • Anyone who wants to practice fixing bugs


How It Works

Admins upload buggy code in various languages (Python, C, Java, HTML). Students get one attempt per question to submit their fixes. Correct submissions earn points and help climb the leaderboard.
